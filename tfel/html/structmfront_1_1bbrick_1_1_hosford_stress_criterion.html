Detailed Description

This class describes the Hosford stress criterion defined by:

\[ \sigma_{\mathrm{eq}}^{H}=\sqrt[a]{\Frac{1}{2}\paren{ \left|\sigma_{1}-\sigma_{2}\right|^{a}+ \left|\sigma_{1}-\sigma_{3}\right|^{a}+ \left|\sigma_{2}-\sigma_{3}\right|^{a}}} \]

where \(\sigma_{1}\), \(\sigma_{2}\) and \(\sigma_{3}\) are the eigenvalues of the stress tensor \(\underline{\sigma}\).
