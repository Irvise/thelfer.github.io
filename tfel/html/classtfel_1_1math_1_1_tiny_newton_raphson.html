template<unsigned short N, typename T, typename F>
class tfel::math::TinyNewtonRaphson< N, T, F >

Implements the Newton-Raphson algorithm when the size N of the problem is known at compile-time. One wish to solve the problem:

\[\vec{f}\left(\vec{x}\right)=\vec{0}\]

where \(\displaystyle\vec{f}:R^{N}\rightarrow R^{N}\). if \(\displaystyle\vec{x}^{n}\) is an estimation of the solution, \(\displaystyle\vec{x}^{n+1}\) will be given by:

\[\displaystyle\vec{x}^{n+1}=\vec{x}^{n}-J^{-1}\vec{f}\left(\vec{x}^{n}\right)\]

where \( J \) is the jacobien matrix of \( f \) at \(\left(\displaystyle\vec{x}^{n}\right)\) defined by \( J_{ij} = \displaystyle\frac{\displaystyle\partial f_{i}}{\displaystyle\partial x_{j}}\).
