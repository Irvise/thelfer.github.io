Detailed Description

describe an general viscoplastic flow.

\[ \underline{\dot{\varepsilon}}^{\mathrm{vp}}= f\left(\phi\left(\underline{\sigma}-\displaystyle\sum_{i}\underline{X}_{i}\right)-R\left(p\right)\right)^{n} \frac{\partial \phi_{f}}{\partial \underline{\sigma}} \]

where:

  • \(f\) is a function describing the flow intensity
  • \(<.>\) are the Macaulay brackets
  • \(\phi\) is the stress criterion
  • \(\phi_{f}\) is the flow criterion
  • \(\underline{X}_{i}\) is the \(i^{\mathrm{th}}\) back stress
  • \(R\left(p\right)\) is the isotropic hardening rule
